Chicken Thai Basil Stir-Fry

Description:

Savor the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia with our Chicken Thai Basil Stir-Fry, a tantalizing dance of aromatic herbs, tender chicken, and crisp vegetables.
This dish is a harmonious fusion of savory and spicy, designed to transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand.
Thin slices of succulent chicken are wok-seared to perfection, infusing each bite with a rich, smoky essence.
The ensemble of garlic and red chili adds a fragrant kick, while the medley of colorful vegetables—bell peppers, snap peas, and julienned carrots—provides a delightful crunch.
Elevating the experience is the umami-laden sauce, a blend of soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and a hint of sweetness. As the wok sizzles, fresh Thai basil leaves are introduced, releasing their aromatic aroma and adding a herbal dimension to the symphony of flavors.
This vibrant stir-fry is a visual feast, bursting with colors and textures that promise a journey to culinary bliss.
Served over a bed of steamed jasmine rice and garnished with lime wedges, each plate is a celebration of balance and boldness.
Whether you're a Thai cuisine enthusiast or a newcomer to its exquisite flavors, our Chicken Thai Basil Stir-Fry invites you to experience the excitement of Southeast Asian gastronomy in the comfort of your own home.
Prepare to be enchanted by this fusion of freshness and spice that defines the essence of Thai cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced (adjust to taste)
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas, ends trimmed
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving
  • Lime wedges, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare Ingredients:
    • Thinly slice the chicken,mince the garlic, and prepare the vegetables.

  2. Heat the wok or skillet:
    • Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat

  3. Sauté Aromatics:
    • Add minced garlic and sliced red chilli to the hot oil. Sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Cook chicken:
    • Add the sliced chicken to the wok. Stir-fry until the chicken is cooked through and lightly browned

  5. Add vegetables:
    • Add bell pepper,snap peas,and julienned carrot the wok. Stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  6. Prepare Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir to combine.

  7. Add thai basil:
    • Once the vegetables are cooked, add fresh thai Basil leaves to the wok.Stir until the basil wilts and becomes aromatic.

  8. Check seasosing:
    • Taste and adjust the seasoning, adding more soy sauce or fish sauce if needed.

  9. Serve:
    • Serve the chicken Thai Basil stir-fry over cooked jasmine rice. Garnish with lime wedges

  10. Enjoy:
    • Squeeze lime over the dish before eating. Enjoy this flavorful and aromatic Chicken Thai Basil Stir-Fry!
